Instructions for Image Upload

This application recognizes four specific hand gestures (thumbs down, victory, thumbs up, and pointing up) from uploaded images. It processes these images using the MediaPipe library to detect gestures and annotate hand landmarks, providing a clear visual output.

To use the Hand Gesture Recognizer, please upload images of the following hand gestures:
  1. Pointing Up
  2. Thumbs Down
  3. Thumbs Up
  4. Victory Sign

Ensure that:

  • The hand is clearly visible in the image.
  • The gesture is clearly recognizable.
  • The background is plain or does not interfere with the gesture visibility.
  • The images are in PNG, JPG, or JPEG format.
After taking the photos, visit the upload page to upload your images and see the gesture recognition results!
