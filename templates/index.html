Instructions for Image Upload

To use the Hand Gesture Classifier, please upload images of the following hand gestures:

  1. Pointing Up: Take a clear photo of a hand making the "pointing up" gesture.
  2. Thumbs Down: Take a clear photo of a hand making the "thumbs down" gesture.
  3. Thumbs Up: Take a clear photo of a hand making the "thumbs up" gesture.
  4. Victory Sign: Take a clear photo of a hand making the "victory" or "peace" sign gesture.

  • The hand is clearly visible in the image.
  • The gesture is clearly recognizable.
  • The background is plain or does not interfere with the gesture visibility.
  • The images are in PNG, JPG, or JPEG format.
After taking the photos, visit the upload page to upload your images and see the gesture recognition results!
